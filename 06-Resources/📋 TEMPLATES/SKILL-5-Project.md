## 🤝 Ethics Checkpoint

> *Before finalizing the project, students should consider:*

- Could this project produce **biased** results? How? What would you do about it?
- Who could be **harmed** by this technology if it was used irresponsibly?
- What **data privacy** concerns exist in this project?
