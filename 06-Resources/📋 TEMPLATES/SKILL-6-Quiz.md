### Scoring Rubric

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **4 — Excellent** | Takes a clear position. Supports it with 2+ specific, accurate reasons. Acknowledges complexity or a counterargument. |
| **3 — Good** | Takes a position. Gives 2 reasons but they may be vague or lack detail. |
| **2 — Developing** | Takes a position but only 1 reason, or reasons are inaccurate/too general. |
| **1 — Beginning** | No clear position, or answer is too brief to evaluate. |
| **0** | No response, off-topic, or plagiarized. |
